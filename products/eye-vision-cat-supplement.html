No. The ingredients are chosen to encourage calmness and relaxation without sedation. Your cat will remain alert and active, just more settled.

Yes. It is suitable for any cat over 6 months old. For kittens under 6 months, please speak with your vet for age-specific advice.

If your cat has an existing medical condition or is under veterinary care, we recommend checking with your vet before introducing any new supplement. This ensures it works well alongside their ongoing treatment plan.

Can this be used alongside other C9 supplements?

Yes. Our products are designed to complement one another, so this calming blend can be used alongside other supplements in the Cloud9 range.

Absolutely. This formula is suitable for daily use, and for best results it should be given consistently over the long term. The short-term ingredients offer gentle support in the moment, while the ongoing ingredients contribute to balance and resilience over time.

How long does it take to see results?

Every cat is different. Some owners notice changes fairly soon, while for others it may take regular use over time to see the full benefit. Consistency is key to helping your cat get the most from the supplement.
